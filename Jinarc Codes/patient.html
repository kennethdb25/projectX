What is JINARC®?

TOP
  • JINARC® is a medication that is prescribed to adults who have a high risk of rapidly progressing Autosomal Dominant Polycystic Kidney Disease (ADPKD) in order to slow down the decline of their kidney function.
  • It has not been determined whether JINARC® is both safe & effective for children.

Who should not take JINARC®?

TOP

Do not take JINARC®, if you:

have a history of liver problems*

*This excludes those with polycystic liver disease who have liver problems.

have high or low sodium levels

are allergic to Tolvaptan

cannot feel thirst or unable to drink

are dehydrated

are unable to urinate

How do I enroll in the JINARC® RMP?

TOP

To enroll in the JINARC® RMP and start taking JINARC®, you need to do the following:

  1. Agree to undergo Liver Function Test before starting and regularly during treatment to monitor your liver health.
  2. Contact JINARC® Patient Support Team to enroll in the JINARC® RMP.

What do I need to do while taking JINARC®?

TOP

To reduce the risk of liver problems while taking JINARC®, your healthcare provider will check your liver function by doing a blood test:

  1. Before you start taking JINARC®
